About AG Gee

A group of Young AG Computer Scientists got together to honor God through their rare skill by connecting all churches in AG Worldwide together in one single platform in an aim to promote the shared gospel to a larger audience and give our Great Men Of God In AG a loud voice in this vast World. In 2009 The Idea AG Gee came to a devoted Youth in AG who was a Computer Programmer. His original plan to build a project for God that would connect the Entire Church Of God, all Denominations Worldwide into a single platform so as to promote God's Message to the entire World with just few clicks. The sole aim of this project is using today's technology which is social media to promote God's contents and educate the World about Jesus.
